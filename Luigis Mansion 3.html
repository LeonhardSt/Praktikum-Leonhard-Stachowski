Now we come to a completely different topic. A topic which is about video games.
In this case: Luigis Mansion 3

What is Luigis Mansion 3? Luigis Mansion 3 is a game about the famous video game character Luigi from the Super Mario Bros Series.

As you've probably already guessed, there are 3 different parts of the game, but in the following i will first describe the concept of the game
and after this i will describe the third part more detailed.

The game concept

How does this game work?

Basically, you are a ghost Hunter and you play as Luigi. With your ghosthunter tool, called Schreckweg-Flu in german, you hunt different
ghosts and try too free your Friends Mario Peach and Toad from the Evil Ghost king Buu-Huu.

The location is alway a haunted place like a Big house or a Hotel.

The Third Part

The Story

Luigi, his brother Mario, her Friend Peach and some Toads, won a vacation in a really big Hotel.

After getting there and checking in everybody went to his own room to slepp a bit after the long trip.
But in the night Luigi gets waken up by a scream of peach. He is really scared and walks out of his room to inspect the hallway
and maybe find out what is happening. The location also changed from a golden shining hotel to a really dark and dirty hotel where you dont want to spent your time.
After Luigi inspected all the rooms of his fellow friends, he gets confronted with King Buu-Huu, who trapped his friends in paintings. Shortly before he can catch Luigi,
can escape and rescues his self in a Laundry slide. This Laundry slide gets him directly into the basement where The game starts. This is the story so far, the action goes
on with Luigi collecting his ghost hunting tools and trying to rescue his friends.

The Map

The Hotel is seperated in 17 different Floors with a different topic and a Boss For Each, to make progress you have to defeat a boss and get the
elevator button he carries with him. With this button you can go to the next floor and go on. The Order you do the floors is not completely from the bottom to the top.

B2: Basement 2 here is the Water and energy supply of the Hotel located and te boss of this floor is a facillity manager

B1: Basement 1 here is the Garage and the Laundry room of the Hotel located, the boss of this Floor is the Hotel receptionist

1st+2nd Floor: The first and the second floor are mixed together and are shown on the same map. On the first floor there is the big Lobby of the Hotel and on the second floor
there are some rooms and the dining room with the kitchen of the Hotel. Here may be 2 floors but there is only one boss on floor 2. This Boss is the Chef of the Hotel.

3rd Floor: The third floor of the Hotel is a shopping center with a big diversity of Stores such as a hairdresser or a souvenir shop.The boss of this
floor is the night Guard of the Shopping mall.

4th Floor: The fourth floor ist a big concert Area with a grand hall. In this hall you have to fight the boss which is a musician that plays piano

5th Floor: On the fith floor there are the VIP suites where Luigi and his friends had there rooms before nearly all of them got trapped inside a painting
on this floor there is no boss because you get the elevator button at the beginning of the game.

6th Floor: The sixth floor of the game is a big castle of a knight with a big arena in the center. On this floor you have to face a king in the bossfight.

7th Floor: The seventh floor is big garden with lots of plants. Some of this plants want to eat you alive so you have to be carefull. In this bossfight,
you have to face the gardener of the hotel that takes care of the variety of plants in the 7th floor.

8th Floor: The eifht floor is a big film set. On this floor you dont have to do a usual bossfight, but a bossfight where you as Luigi fight against godzilla.
But there is a boss ghost who is called Morty. Morty is the film director and you can decide if you want to catch him or not.

9th Floor: The nineth Floor is a Museum with a big variety of Dinosaur skeletts. In case of this, you have to face a skeletton T-Rex in the bossfigt.
With the bossfight going on, you realize that the boss is not really the T-Rex, but a Ghost who was inside the T-Rex.

10th Floor: The First floor with two numbers in its name, is basically a big Desert. In the center of this desert is a big Pyramid whith a Ghost inside of it.
The ghost inside of the pyramid, is the bosss of this floor. And how could it be different: the boss is the ghost of Kleopatra.

11th Floor: The eleventhh floor is a big Labyrint of Rooms designed by the three boss ghosts: Nikki, Lindsey and Ginny. These three little ghosts are wizards that
that want to distract you, on your way to free your friends, with their tricks.

12th Floor: The 12th floor has a pirate theme and in cause of this, there is a big bar on this floor where you can rest and take a drink. But the way more important
is behind the bar. There is a ship that you can enter. After you entered the ship you will quickly realize what awaits you on this ship. The boss of this floor
a big pirate shark.

13th Floor: The thirteenth floor is for all the people who want to do Sports until they die, because the 13th floor is a fitnessstudio. The boss ghost of floor 13 did
sports until he died, he is a swimmer who will fight with you in a swimming pool.

14th Floor: After a wet intermezzo with Johnny Deepend, you have to show your dance skills in the disco on floor 14. After enough practice you have to face DJ Phantasmagloria
a DJ as the name says and the boss of Floor 14.

15th Floor: Now you are on the home stretch. When you finished your epic dance battek on floor 14 you have to visit the ultimate luxury VIP Suite of King Buu-Huu. You maybe think
now I have ti fight the King himself? No first you have to eliminate the Boss of the Hotel: Helen Gravely.

Enemies

In this game there are also a few normal ghost that you have to fight while you are rescueing your fellow friends.
In the following you can find a list of these ghosts and their special abilities.


Normal/Blue Ghost: This ghost is the Basic of the Basic, he has a normal amount of life points and no special abilites. Sometimes he wears a hat or sunglesses which protect him from being flashed.



Strong/Red Ghost: This ghost is the second one you encounter after the blue ghost. He has twice as much life points as the blue ghost and he has a charge attack.
The red ghost can also wear a hat or sunglasses which protect him.

Sneaky/Purple Ghost: The Purple Ghost has a little bit more health pints than the blue one but he has also a special ability. He can make himself invisible and
then he tries to grab luigi from behind to catch him and hold him.

Poltergeist/Yellow ghost: The yellow ghost does not have that much health points, but he can hide in objects and he can scare Luigi when he jumps out of these objects
